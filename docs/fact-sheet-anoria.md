## C · Features & capabilities

- **Core features (as described by the company):**
  - Reads emotional state in real time and produces a **"Flow Score"** built from three components: **Energy, Mood, and Focus**.
  - Powered by **SOMI** ("state-of-mind inference model"), described as using a neuroscience framework to correlate **~150 audio and biometric signals** (including heart rate and HRV) to infer what the wearer is feeling, why, and what to do about it.
  - Contextual integrations described in company/marketing material: connects to **calendar** (to show which meetings drain you) and **music** (to track what shifts your state); builds a personal map of emotional patterns over time.
- **Recently shipped / status:** Pre-launch. As of mid-2026 the company describes itself as roughly "two months in," with prototypes being tested by early users; **early-access pre-orders open by invitation**. No shipping consumer product confirmed yet.
- **Things they explicitly do NOT do / common misconceptions:** This is **not** an established, widely shipping wearable — it is a 2026 early-stage startup product. It is **not** a body-recovery/sleep tracker in the Whoop/Oura mold; its stated focus is **emotional state (EQ)**, not recovery or sleep scores. *(Capability claims beyond the above are unverified — leave blank.)*
- **Key integrations:** Calendar and music integrations are described by the company (specific named third-party services not confirmed).
